Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Please follow these guidelines for a secure and enjoyable rafting experience.

Wear Life Jacket

Always wear a certified life jacket throughout the rafting session.

Helmet is Mandatory

Protect your head ‚Äî a helmet must be worn at all times (we'll provide).

Follow Instructions

Listen carefully and follow all instructions from your guide.

Swimming Skills (Optional)

Not mandatory, but basic swimming knowledge is helpful.

No Alcohol / Drugs

Consumption of alcohol or drugs before rafting is strictly prohibited.

Basic Fitness

Rafting is a physical activity. Ensure you're reasonably fit.
